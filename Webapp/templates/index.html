The Mycotoxin and Nutrition Analysis platform

High-end analytical testing and analysis

Made In Africa, For You.

The Mycotoxin and Nutrition Analysis platform at the BecA-ILRI Hub offers analytical testing services and support in food safety, food nutrition and related agricultural applications. The platform is equipped with high-end analytical testing equipment used in analysis of both inorganic and organic compounds of interest in bioscience applications.

The platform is open to researchers, regional research organizations or international research centers operating in Africa.

Why choose us?

We are part of The International Livestock Research Institute (ILRI) works with partners worldwide to enhance the roles that livestock play in food security and poverty alleviation, principally in Africa and Asia.

The platform currently serves and has the potential to serve Scientists across different research themes including:

Food toxicology scientists and epidemiologists.

  • Evaluation of contamination of food and animal feed by mycotoxin from various mycotoxigenic fungus
  • Prevalence of adulteration of milk
Crop improvement scientists i.e. plant breeders
  • Screening of various bioactive components of interest to plant breeders
  • Breeding for elimination or reduction of antinutritive properties
  • Evaluating the effect of genetic breeding on various bioactive components
Food science and nutrition scientists:
  • Profiling of nutritional components in various foodstuffs
  • Assessing the effects of various food processing techniques on the nutritional and antinutritional factors
